What exactly is the free audit?

A personal review of your online presence across the five points listed above. You'll get a plain-English, one-page report — what's working, what's costing you customers, and the first three things to fix. No automated PDF, no sales trap.

How fast will I get it?

The free audit lands within 24 hours of your request. The paid $99 done-for-you audit is delivered within 48 hours, with a recorded walkthrough.

Do you work with businesses outside my area?

Yes — Summit Studio is fully remote and works with local businesses worldwide. Everything is delivered online, and calls happen over video.

What's included in a monthly plan?

It depends on the tier. Base Camp keeps you consistent and visible on social; Summit adds paid ads; Peak is full-service with unlimited platforms, website upkeep, and strategy. All plans include reporting so you can see what's working. Ad spend is separate and paid directly to the platform.

How does payment work?

Projects $500 and under are paid in full upfront. Projects over $500 take a 60% deposit to begin, with the remaining 40% due at delivery. Need it rushed? Priority (48-hour) is +25% and The 24 Summit (24-hour) is +50%.

Will you try to sell me a big retainer?

No. The audit is genuinely free and useful on its own. If a monthly plan makes sense for where you're headed, we'll talk about it — only if you want to.
